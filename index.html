About

Redacted lets you easily blur/pixelate/black out parts of an image online — without being tracked.

Will you have access to my image?

No, the image you "upload" will not be sent to a server. All the magic is done directly in your browser. Which means it also works offline.

Tracking

No tracking is being used.
